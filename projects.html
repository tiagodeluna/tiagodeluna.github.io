Unreal Games.

The Unreal Engine is a game engine developed by Epic Games and used by many game developers nowaday. Here are some games I've been working using the Unreal 4 engine/editor.

MUNA

MUNA is a sci-fi 3D sidescroller inspired by classic platform games like Megaman, Metroid, and Castlevania, plus some other games and movies such as Portal, Tron, Matrix, and Robocop.

This game was created as part of a jam in the course of Digital Game Development at SENAI. The team was composed of six people, and I was responsible for the project management, script, cadence and the design of some levels.



Torch of Dungeon

Torch of Dungeon is a third person maze exploration game that combines elements of fantasy with a steampunk look.

It is a project developed during the course of Digital Game Development by the whole class. I worked as a level designer on this project.



Unity.

Unity is a cross-platform game engine developed by Unity Technologies and used to develop video games for PC, consoles, mobile devices and websites. This section lists the games I've worked using this platform.

A Planet of Apes

A Planet of Apes is a 2D roguelike game with pixelated graphics and levels generated procedurally, developed in three days for a hackathon. The team was composed of two programmers and a graphic artist.

The game's thematic and aesthetics are freely inspired by the Planet of the Apes series.
